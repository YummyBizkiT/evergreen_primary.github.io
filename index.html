Welcome!


At Evergreen Primary School we make every effort to ensure our school is a self-reliant learning institution. It is our intention to provide a relevant and quality learning experience to learners and stakeholders in the community.



Without any discrimination we will do this by:

  • Making teaching and learning our first priority in our school.

  • Preparing our learners to become responsible members of society.

  • Providing opportunities for our educators to develop and improve their teaching skills.

  • Getting support and commitment from the community so that we can upgrade and develop the school environment and also share the school resources with the community.


We hope to achieve these goals through dynamic teamwork, total commitment and dedication.
